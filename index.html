The 4 Moves
1
Ovulation · Days 14–16
Plan a FUN date when she's ovulating
Peak confidence, peak connection. She's magnetic, social, adventurous. Take her somewhere that requires effort — climbing, a new restaurant, a drive. She'll remember it. So will you.
2
Follicular Phase · Days 5–13
Workout together when her period is over
Estrogen is rising, energy is building. She's motivated, strong, mentally sharp. Hit the gym, go for a run. This is her power phase — match her energy.
3
Luteal Phase · Days 17–28
Cook & have deep conversations in her luteal phase
She's turning inward. Less social, more reflective. This is when she needs real connection, not distractions. Cook together, talk, listen more than you speak. Don't take moodiness personally.
4
Period Phase · Days 1–4
Buy her favourite meal when she's on her period
She's losing energy and iron. Don't ask what she wants — just get it. Reduce friction, increase comfort. Heating pad, her food, quiet company. Be present, not loud.
